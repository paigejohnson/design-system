About Me

“Genius is the ability to reduce the complicated to the simple.”

- C.W. Ceram

My name is Paige Johnson and I am currently a graduate student at DePaul University. I will be graduating in 2024 with a Master’s of Science in Human-Computer Interaction. I am passionate about creating effective, user-centered solutions that focus on simplicity and balance while incorporating user testing measures to consistently make improvements.

When I am not busy working or studying, I love embarking on new adventures including but limited to cross-country road trips, exploring new cities/countries, going to see concerts with friends, and grabbing a bite at new restaurants. In addition to this, some of my favorite pastimes include reading, writing, artwork, baking, and snowboarding.
